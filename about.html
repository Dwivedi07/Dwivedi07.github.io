Hi there! Thank you for your interest in my profile!

I'm a master's student in Aeronautics and Astronautics Engineering at Stanford University. I am currently doing research in the Autonomous Systems Lab advised by Professor Marco Pavone.

Before Stanford, I completed my B. Tech. in Mechanical Engineering with Honors and a minor in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Indian Institute of Technology Bombay, India. During this time, I worked with Professor Dwaipayan Mukherjee in the Controls and Computing Lab. My research interests lie at the intersection of Control Theory, Machine Learning, and Decision Making, with a particular focus on autonomous driving and space vehicles.
