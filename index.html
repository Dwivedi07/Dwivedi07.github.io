Arpit Dwivedi

I'm a master's student in Aeronautics and Astronautics Engineering at Stanford University.

Before Stanford, I completed my B. Tech. in Mechanical Engineering with Honours and a minor in Artificial Intelligence at IIT Bombay, where I worked with Professor Dwaipayan Mukherjee in the Controls and Computing Lab.
