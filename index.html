Decentralized Control Strategy for Path-Following in Snake Robots
Arpit Dwivedi, Hemanta Hazarika, Dwaipayan Mukherjee, and Debasattam Pal
International Conference on Control, Decision and Information Technologies (CoDIT), 2025

This project involved the design, fabrication, and integration of a modular snake robot capable of locomotion. The robot's kinematic and dynamic models were developed to analyze its motion and interactions, followed by accessibility analysis of the fabricated design. \ A more general model of the snake robot has been studied that incorporates an eccentric point where the friction force acts on each module. Moreover, a decentralized path-following control algorithm has been proposed for our general model. The proposed algorithm exploits local link-level autonomy and helps the robot maneuver smoothly for any general path curve.

Coverage Maximization for UAV Surveillance on Non-convex Domains using Genetic Algorithm
Arpit Dwivedi, Chinmay Pimpalkhare
NCMDAO, December 2023.
This work develops a UAV surveillance framework for disaster management, focusing on maximizing coverage in non-convex and disconnected regions. The framework processes input images, extracts boundaries, and reduces complexity by approximating space as a union of polygons. A genetic algorithm with Monte Carlo sampling optimizes sensor configurations, leveraging problem geometry for efficient coverage.

Jet Impingement Heat Transfer
